Index / Tower strategies

Strategies control the behavior of your towers. Strategies cannot be changed while building the tower, you have to wait until it is finished building.

Strategy

Target
Weak The creep with the least hit points in range
Hard The creep with the most hit points in range.
Fastest The fastest creep in range
Close The creep closest to the tower
Farthest The creep which left behind the longest way on the map
Lock Locks on a selected target until it is out of range
