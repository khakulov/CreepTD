Index / Game modes

ALL vs ALL

Creeps are sent to every opponent. If a creep gets through a player's game board, it appears again at the beginning.


Send to next

Creeps are sent to the next player (clockwise) and will run through the opponents' game boards sequentially.


Send to random

Simmilar to "Send to next", but the player receiving a sent or transfered creep is randomly chosen.


Team 2vs2

Players 1 and 2 (top) play against players 3 and 4 (bottom). Players 1 and 2 send their creeps to player 3, players 3 and 4 to player 1.
